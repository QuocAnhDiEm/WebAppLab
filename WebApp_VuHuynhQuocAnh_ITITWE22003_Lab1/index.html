Welcome to the IU Student Lounge

The IU Student Lounge is your dedicated space for relaxation and quick energy boosts. Stop by to sample our selection of healthy drinks, teas, and coffees, or utilize our space for group projects and collaborative study.

Our commitment: at IU, we're dedicated to fostering a supportive environment for every student. Whether you're pulling an all-nighter or just grabbing a quick break, you'll find everything you need to succeed. Your well-being is our priority.

Ready to check us out? We're located conveniently on campus. Find detailed directions and our operating hours on the student portal.
